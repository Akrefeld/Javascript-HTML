Please read the following instructions carefully!

We will ask you to choose one out of two used cars that you prefer in the following task.

To inform your choice you will receive the following information in a table like the following:

For each car you receive the information whether:
  • the price is below 5’000 CHF
  • the mileage is below 100’000 km
  • the car has a registration date after 2014
  • the car has seat heating

Asterisks illustrate YES the criterion is met and hyphens illustrate NO, the criterion is not met.

In the presented example, Car A doesn't cost less than 5’000 CHF, doesn't have a mileage below 100’000 km and its first registration was before 2014. However, it does have seat heating.

Car B costs less than 5’000 CHF, doesn't have a mileage below 100’000 km, was initially registered after 2014 and has seat heating.

Now you are asked to decide which of the two cars you would prefer.


If you feel ready to make this choice click on the continue button.

Some of you will then also receive an additional recommendation by a car dealer before they can make their choice. After you have made you choice, we will finally ask you for your gender, age group and student status.

Have you understood the instructions? Please ask the experimenter for help if you have additional questions. If not, click the continue button to start the experiment!
